# Algorithm – Tower of Hanoi

## Step 1
Start the program.

## Step 2
Read the number of disks.

## Step 3
Call the recursive function.

## Step 4
If there is only one disk:
- Move it from Source to Destination.

## Step 5
Move n−1 disks from Source to Auxiliary.

## Step 6
Move the largest disk from Source to Destination.

## Step 7
Move n−1 disks from Auxiliary to Destination.

## Step 8
Repeat until all disks are moved.

## Step 9
Display all moves.

## Step 10
Stop the program.

---

## Time Complexity

| Operation | Complexity |
|----------|------------|
| Tower of Hanoi | O(2ⁿ) |

---

## Space Complexity

**O(n)**
